Team-based FPS with destructible environments

CLASSES

LIGHT

Speed and agility define the Light class. Equipped with lightweight armor and rapid-fire weapons, Lights excel in hit-and-run tactics, flanking maneuvers, and quick objective captures. Their mobility allows them to navigate the battlefield swiftly, making them ideal for reconnaissance and surprise attacks.

MEDIUM

The Medium class strikes a balance between speed and firepower. With moderate armor and versatile weapons, Mediums can adapt to various combat situations, providing support to teammates while also engaging enemies effectively. They are well-rounded fighters, capable of holding their own in most scenarios.

HEAVY

Heavy class operators are the tanks of the battlefield. With high health pools and powerful weaponry, Heavies can absorb damage and deal significant firepower. They excel in holding positions and providing cover for teammates, making them essential for team-based strategies.
